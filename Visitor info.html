Everything You Need for Festival Weekend

October 11 & 12, 2024  |  Downtown Magnolia Springs, GA

Visitor Information

  • Dates and Times

    Friday from 12:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. and Saturday from 12:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.

  • Location

    Magnolia Springs City Park, 1214 Park Road, Magnolia Springs, GA 30001.

  • Family Friendly

    Enjoy live music, games, crafts, food trucks, vendors, and activities for all ages.

  • Free Admission

    Admission is free. Bring money for food, shopping, and select activities.

Parking and Accessibility

Parking

Free parking will be available in marked lots near the festival. Follow signs and parking attendants.

Accessible Parking

Accessible parking will be available near the main entrance for vehicles with a valid permit.

Guest Services

Visit the information booth near the main entrance for directions, lost items, or assistance.

What to Bring

Bring comfortable shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, water, and a chair or blanket.

Important Notes

Are pets allowed?

Leashed pets and trained service animals are welcome. Owners must clean up after their animals.

What happens if it rains?

Activities may continue during light rain but may be delayed or canceled during severe weather.

Are restrooms available?

Standard and accessible restrooms will be available throughout the festival grounds.

Festival Text Updates

Sign up to receive schedule, parking, and weather updates by text message.

Arrive early for parking and performances.

Check social media for updates.

Admission is free for all visitors.
