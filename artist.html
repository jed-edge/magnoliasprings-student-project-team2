Featured Artists

Clara Whitmore

Clara creates delicate watercolor florals inspired by Southern gardens and seasonal landscapes. Her work blends magnolia blossoms, soft colors, and autumn details into calm, elegant compositions.

Elias Mercer

Elias specializes in whimsical festival illustrations filled with glowing lanterns, colorful tents, and lively fall scenery. His storybook-style paintings capture the warmth and charm of community celebrations.

June Holloway

June paints cozy autumn still lifes featuring pumpkins, flowers, rustic décor, and natural textures. Her folk-art-inspired work uses cheerful colors and handcrafted details to celebrate the comforts of fall.

Theo Bennett

Theo creates peaceful landscape paintings inspired by parks, lakes, and small-town festivals. His soft brushwork and warm seasonal colors give each scene a nostalgic, welcoming atmosphere.
